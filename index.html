Welcome to my web page!

I am a PhD candidate in the Information Security Research Group at the Computer Science Department of University College London. My supervisors are Dr. Emiliano De Cristofaro and Dr. Gordon Ross.

My research interests include applied cryptography, privacy-enhancing technologies, distributed systems as well as privacy-friendly analytics and machine learning applications to security.

Short Bio

I received my BSc and MSc from the Computer Engineering and Informatics Department (CEID) at University of Patras, Greece. During my MSc, I worked as a researcher at the Computer Technology Institute and Press Diophantus (CTI) in the EU FP7 project ABC4Trust. More details about me can be found in my CV.
