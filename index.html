Welcome to my web page!

I am a Post-Doctoral researcher in the Laboratory for Data Security at the School of Computer and Communication Sciences of École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne working under the supervision of Prof. Jean-Pierre Hubaux.

My research interests include privacy-enhancing technologies and applied cryptography, and I enjoy studying problems at the intersection of big data analytics and security or privacy.

Short Bio

I received my PhD from the Information Security Research Group at the Computer Science Department of University College London. My supervisors were Prof. Emiliano De Cristofaro and Dr. Gordon Ross. During my PhD, I also did research internships at the Alan Turing Institute and Telefonica Research Barcelona. I received my BSc and MSc from the Department of Computer Engineering and Informatics of University of Patras in Greece. During my MSc, I also worked as a researcher at the Computer Technology Institute and Press Diophantus in the EU FP7 project ABC4Trust. More details about me can be found in my CV.
